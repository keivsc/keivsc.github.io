What is ValTracker?

ValTracker is a VALORANT Tracker that can display players' ranks, K/D, win rate and much more to come! It also allows you to look at your match histories and look at other players' profile with the search tab! Tracklorant has no limits and no private profiles! You will be able to access every players' profile!

ValTracker Is Currently a Work In Progress

Here is a screenshot of EARLY development

ValTracker is currently still work in progress and will include overview of your account stats such as your KDA and Match History. With ValTracker, private profiles are not a thing! All accounts are available for the public to access

Features

Stats Overview

Display for your KDA, Accuracy,  
Match History

Live Match Stats

Be able to get user's live match stats with rank, all modes available with no private profiles

User Search

Search up user's stats by their name to check their
Stats and match history
